3.BVSR Constructions Pvt. Ltd.

This particular construction company has established itself on a national level for its strong reputation, fast-growing clientele, and leading projects. The scale of their projects ranges from medium to large civil projects as well. With Civil Engineering being their main focus, the company works towards the concept, design, construction, and commissioning of EPC and BOT modes. Their projects revolve around highways, Earth dams, Spillways, bridges, roads, and more. The company runs on the motto of financial soundness, competence, quality, and safety when it comes to their construction methodology. They believe their cost-effective systems are the reason behind their successful projects.
