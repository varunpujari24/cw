2. The Phoenix Group

The Phoenix Group is an organization that has its fingers in various jars of Realty, Automobiles, Mining, and Power. Despite all the ventures they go on, The Phoenix Group stays loyal to its primary growth engine, that being Realty. They showcase a portfolio consisting of projects built-in luxury and comfort along with testimonies from numerous clients regarding their work. The Group touts being way ahead when it comes to the use of technology in their projects along with quality and size. This construction company works towards creating diverse global life spaces from homes and residencies to offices and commercial spaces. Some of their on-going projects include the Gold Edge in Gachibowli and the Halcyon in Jubilee Hills.
