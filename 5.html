5.My Home Constructions

The brainchild of Dr. Rameswar Rao, My Home Constructions is part of the My Home Group that saw its establishment in 1981. The company believes in the marriage of a great home with a great lifestyle. This company saw the establishment of 16 gated communities across the city. My Home Hub, My Home Rainbow, My Home Navadweepa, My Home Tycoon are among these 16 addresses. They have been known to be one of the top construction companies in Hyderabad and their work speaks for itself. My Home Constructions dedicate all their accomplishments and work to their six principles. These principles are clear documentation, desirable destinations, happy communities, secure environment, superior living, and timely delivery.
